Hello! I'm Siwapon Chaisuriya . I graduated from Chiang Mai University in the College of Arts, Media, and Technology, majoring in Digital Game I’m a recent graduate with a passion for game design, drawing, and technical artistry. I specialize in game design, where I enjoy crafting immersive experiences that engage players.
